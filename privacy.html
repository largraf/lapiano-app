Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Overview

Lapiano is designed with privacy as a core principle. The application operates entirely on your device and does not require a user account.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information.

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics
  • No advertising
  • No tracking
  • No personal data sent to the developer

Microphone

Lapiano uses your device's microphone only for real-time musical note detection. Audio is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Bluetooth

Bluetooth permissions are used exclusively to connect to compatible MIDI devices. Bluetooth is not used for location tracking.

Local Storage

Practice settings and progress are stored only on your device. Depending on your Android backup settings, this data may be included in encrypted device backups managed by your device or Google account.

Third-Party Services

Lapiano does not use third-party analytics, advertising, cloud services, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Because Lapiano does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page by updating the "Last updated" date.
