What is Lapiano?

Lapiano helps you practice reading piano sheet music using either a MIDI keyboard or an acoustic piano.

Everything happens locally on your device. No account is required, no advertisements are shown, and no analytics are collected.

Features

  • 🎼 Learn sheet music
  • 🎹 MIDI keyboard support
  • 🎙️ Acoustic piano note detection
  • 📱 Fully offline
  • 🔒 Privacy-first
  • 🚫 No ads
  • 🚫 No account
