Lapiano

Learn to read piano sheet music.
Offline. Privacy-first. Free.

About

Lapiano helps you practice reading piano sheet music using either an acoustic piano or a MIDI keyboard.

Everything happens locally on your device. No account is required, no advertisements are shown, and no analytics are collected.

Features

  • 🎼 Learn to read sheet music
  • 🎹 Acoustic piano note detection
  • 🎹 Bluetooth MIDI support
  • 📱 Fully offline
  • 🔒 Privacy-first
  • 🚫 No ads
  • 🚫 No account required
